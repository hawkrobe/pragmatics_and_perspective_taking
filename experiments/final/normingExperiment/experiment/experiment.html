Stanford CoCoLab

In this HIT, you will see a sequence of labels and pictures of objects.

For each pair, you will use a slider bar to rate how well the given label describes the given object: from 'strongly disagree' if the label doesn't match the object at all to 'strongly agree' if the label matches perfectly. For example, in the display below, the highlighted object is definitely a pair of "black sunglasses" so it would be rated very highly.

Occasionally, labels will refer to the object's location in the grid (e.g. "bottom" or "top"), or explicitly compare it to other objects (e.g. "larger" or "smaller"). For these labels, you may find that you need to look at the full grid to know whether the label is good or not. For the vast majority of labels, however, the grid and other objects are irrelevant and can be ignored.

The whole HIT should take no longer than 15 minutes: you will see a progress bar in the top-right corner. Thank you for your work!
